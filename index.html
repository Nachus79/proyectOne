Keys:

  • To go up press "W" or "Up arrow"

  • To go down press "S" or "Down arrow"

  • To go right press "D" or "Right arrow"

  • To go left press "A" or "Left arrow"

  • To fire press "F" or click left mouse button"

  • To pause the game press the button "START/PAUSE" or "P" key.
